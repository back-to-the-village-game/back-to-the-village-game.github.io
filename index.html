Back to the Village

Build your dream farm and enjoy a peaceful life!

Have you ever dreamed of escaping the noisy and polluted metropolis? The hero of Back to the Village takes this step and starts a new life among fields, gardens, and cozy buildings.

Create a farm from scratch that you can truly be proud of: grow crops, care for animals, develop production, and step by step turn a small household into a thriving business. And who knows — maybe this story will inspire your own farm 😉

🌱 Gardening

Plant vegetables, grains, and fruit trees. Harvest a rich crop and unlock new plant species.

🐄 Animal Husbandry

Get cute pets. Don't forget to feed them and collect fresh products.

⚒️ Master of Crafting

Build and develop production buildings. Turn raw materials into valuable goods to grow your farm even faster.

⚖️ Market Relations

Visit the local Market to profitably sell your harvest or buy everything needed for farm improvements.

🏆 Farmer's Book

Become a true collector! Grown plants, crafted goods, bought animals, or built structures are added to your personal record book.

🧘 Stress-Free Game

Eternal summer reigns in Back to the Village. No rush: your crops won't wilt, and animals won't die. Play for your pleasure at any time.

Frequently Asked Questions

Currently, there are 20 levels in the game. After reaching level 20, the game enters freeplay mode — all features remain available as before, but no new quests will appear until the next update.

The daily reward offers a bonus of coins or vouchers every 24 hours. The bonus amount increases each day. If you don't claim the reward within 48 hours of its appearance, the counter will reset to Day 1.

Garden beds and tree holes cannot be moved once placed, but they can be deleted using the corresponding tool. The coins spent on creation will be credited back to your balance. However, any plant growing in it will also be removed without a refund.

Access to purchasing animals is unlocked after completing the specific building quest (coop, sheepfold, or cowshed) and clicking the "Claim Reward" button in the quest list. If you built the structure before the quest appeared, it will count as completed as soon as it shows up in your list.

Animals will not die. An icon will appear showing that they need food, and they will stop producing goods until their feeders are refilled.

No, watering is not necessary. It rains periodically in the game, which automatically waters the plants and speeds up their growth by 20%.
